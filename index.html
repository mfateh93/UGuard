Who we are

Our objective is to build a handheld device and phone application. UGuard, implements a groundbreaking safety protocol to help college students stay safe at their campuses. We are searching for motivated, skillful, and ambitious engineers with a strong background in programming, hardware, and wireless technology. Details are summarized below.

Software Challenges

  • Seperation of concern between the phone and the device
  • Ensuring a secure environment for user data
  • Emergency requests are served in real-time
  • Delivering notification to close contact even when service is unavailable

Hardware Challenges

  • Cellular Network Access
  • Configuring Wireless Hardware
  • Power Management

Proposed Methods
